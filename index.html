CORONA ALERT

corona disease(COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a new virus.The disease causes respiratory illness(like the flu) with symptoms such as a cough,fever and in more severe cases ,difficulty breathing.

What are the symptoms?

Covid-19 syptoms ranges from mild to severe.It takes 2-14 days after exposure for symptoms to develop.Symptoms may include :

COUGH

A dry cough does not produce mucus.According to the United Kingdom’s National Health Service (NHS), if a person notices they are coughing a lot for over an hour, or they have three or more coughing episodes in a day, they may have coronavirus.

FATIGUE

Fatigue is a common symptom of several conditions and serious diseases, but in most cases it is caused by simple lifestyle factors.

FEVER

Doctors consider a temperature of 100.4°F or higher to be a fever.A person with a fever will feel hot to touch on their back or chest.

SHORTEN OF BREATHE

Shortness of breath is a subjective feeling. However, those experiencing shortness of breath may describe it feeling as if they are suffocating, or unable to catch their breath.

HEADACHE

Headaches can sometimes be mild, but in many cases, they can cause severe pain that makes it difficult to concentrate at work and perform other daily activities.

SORE THROAT

Pain in the throat is one of the most common symptoms. It accounts for more than 13 million visits to doctor’s offices each year

ALWAYS WEAR A FACE MASK

Everyone should wear a cloth face cover in public settings and when around people who don’t live in your household, especially when other social distancing measures are difficult to maintain. Cloth face coverings should not be placed on young children under age 2, anyone who has trouble breathing, or is unconscious, incapacitated or otherwise unable to remove the mask without assistance.

STOP TOUCHING FACE EVERYTIME

stop touching your face to minimize spread of coronavirus and other germs

AVOID CLOSE CONTACT

Avoid close contact with people who are sick.If possible, maintain 6 feet between the person who is sick and other household members.Put 6 feet of distance between yourself and people who don’t live in your household.Remember that some people without symptoms may be able to spread virus.
